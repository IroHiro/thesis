❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about TerraSight and how to use the platform.

What is TerraSight?

+
TerraSight is a crop suitability and yield prediction platform that helps farmers make smarter planting decisions using weather, soil, and vegetation data.

How do I use TerraSight?

+
Select your municipality, choose your preferred crop, and click analyze to receive crop suitability scores and yield predictions.

Why is my municipality not listed?

+
The system currently supports Polangui, Oas, Ligao, Malinao, and Pio Duran based on available agricultural datasets.

What crops are supported?

+
TerraSight currently supports Rice and Corn crop analysis.

Where does the data come from?

+
Data comes from weather APIs, vegetation indices (NDVI/EVI), and local agricultural records.
