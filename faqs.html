Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Click a question to view the answer.

What is the Albay Agricultural Monitoring System?

▼

It is a web-based system that visualizes crop distribution and analyzes agricultural data to support climate-adaptive decision-making in Albay.

How to use the Agricultural Climate-Adaptive Prediction System?

▼

1. Go to Homepage.
2. Select your municipality/city from the dropdown menu.
3. Choose a crop (Corn, Rice/Palay, or Banana).
4. Click "Get Prediction" to view suitability and yield forecasts.

Why my municipality is not included?

▼

The system focuses on City of Ligao, Pio Duran, Malinao, Polangui, and Oas. It heavily depended on the data given by the Department of Agriculture.

What crops are monitored?

▼

Corn, Rice/Palay, and Banana are currently monitored in the system.

Where does the data come from?

▼

Data is sourced from the RSBSA Registry and official agricultural records.

Who developed this system?

▼

This system was developed as a thesis project by Computer Science students from Bicol University.

Project: Data-Driven Crop Suitability and Yield Prediction for Climate-Adaptive Agriculture in Albay
