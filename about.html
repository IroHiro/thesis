About Climate-Adaptive Prediction System in Albay

Learn about our mission to visualize and analyze crop distribution across Albay's municipalities.

Our Mission

The Albay Agricultural Monitoring System aims to provide farmers, researchers, and policymakers with clear, data-driven insights into crop distribution and agricultural trends in key municipalities.

By combining geographic visualization and statistical analysis, we help the agricultural community make informed decisions and adapt to changing environmental conditions.

Focus Areas

Our system currently highlights the top 5 municipalities for crop production:

  • City of Ligao
  • Pio Duran
  • Malinao
  • Polangui
  • Oas

We monitor major crops such as Corn, Rice/Palay, and Banana, providing detailed data and insights for each area.

Our Approach

We use data-driven methods, including Random Forest and LSTM models, to analyze crop distribution patterns and forecast trends.

The system is designed to support climate-adaptive agriculture, helping farmers maximize yields while minimizing environmental impact.

About Us

This project is part of the Thesis project of the Computer Science students from Bicol University, dedicated to improving agricultural outcomes through technology and data science.

We collaborate with local stakeholders, researchers, and government agencies to ensure accurate and actionable insights.
