Our Mission

TerraSight aims to empower farmers, researchers, and policymakers with data-driven insights for improved agricultural planning and climate resilience.

Focus Areas

We analyze crop distribution across key municipalities:

  • City of Ligao
  • Pio Duran
  • Malinao
  • Polangui
  • Oas

Technology

The system uses machine learning models such as Random Forest and LSTM to predict crop yield and identify climate-adaptive farming patterns.

About the Team

TerraSight is developed by Computer Science students of Bicol University as part of a thesis project focused on agricultural innovation and sustainability.
